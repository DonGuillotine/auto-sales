DELIVERY INFORMATION

Dapperautoparts.com now offers shipping and delivery of it’s parts.

PLEASE NOTE:

Orders placed after 4pm, will be processed the following day, adding 1 working day to the delivery service. When selecting 'Next Working Day' - Orders placed after 4pm on a Friday, will be delivered on the next working day which is the following Monday. For orders placed on a non working day (Saturday or Sunday) please add 1 working day to your delivery date.

If your item is Over-Weight (Over 100kg) or you are located in a remote area you may incur an additional charge. If this is the case the cost will be automatically calculated by our system at the checkout phase.

Due to the volume of economy deliveries, please allow a full 7 working days before contacting our customer service centre. All delivery times are approximate and can be delayed due to circumstances outside of our control.

No deliveries will be made on Sundays or Bank/ Public Holidays. Delivery timescales will vary for items which are not marked as in-stock.

FURTHER INFORMATION: Bank/ Public Holidays

No deliveries can be made on Bank/ Public Holidays. Any deliveries scheduled to arrive on a Bank/Public Holiday will be delivered the following working day. In addition, operations are closed on Bank/ Public Holidays, so orders placed on a Bank/ Public Holiday will not be dispatched until the following working day.
