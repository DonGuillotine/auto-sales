Therefore, no orders can be cancelled after receiving an electronic order acceptance. In addition, all orders placed between Friday after 5PM to Monday 9AM cannot be canceled, as they are processed over the weekend for shipment.RETURNSWe have a return policy on some stated items and the requirements for a valid returns that must be met follows; It must be within four (4) days from delivery date The item is in new condition (unused). It must part of the listed products which returns are valid on. It is accompanied with a return form Note; all returns that don’t meet this requirements stated above will be automatically declined and sent back.

Should you wish for another refund method, indicate it on the return form.To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase. Make sure serial number of item is included in receipt.RETURN PROCEDURE- Provide your order number, name, email address, phone number and the part number you wish to return, along with an explanation as to why the part is being returned.- Contact us through Mail or Telephone - All returns must be packaged securely.
